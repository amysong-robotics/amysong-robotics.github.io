I am a highly motivated undergraduate researcher majoring in **Robotics Engineering** at the[Southern University of Science and Technology (SUSTech)](https://www.sustech.edu.cn/). 

My research is driven by a deep passion for building **intelligent autonomous systems** that can seamlessly integrate into human environments. Specifically, I am fascinated by the intersection of **safe social interactions** and **dynamic locomotion** in complex, real-world scenarios. My current work focuses on bridging the gap between simulation and reality (**Sim-to-Real transfer**) to develop robust, adaptive control algorithms. 

Ultimately, I aspire to pursue a Ph.D. in Robotics to further push the boundaries of embodied AI.
