I am a highly motivated undergraduate researcher majoring in **Robotics Engineering** at the[Southern University of Science and Technology (SUSTech)](https://www.sustech.edu.cn/) with an overall GPA of 3.85/4.0. 

My research is driven by a deep passion for building **intelligent embodied systems** capable of agile locomotion, dexterous manipulation, and safe interactions in unstructured environments. I possess a **"full-stack" robotics background**, bridging the gap between high-level autonomous algorithms and low-level embedded hardware control.

Currently, my research spans three main areas:
* **Sim-to-Real & Reinforcement Learning:** I have constructed high-fidelity simulation pipelines using **NVIDIA Isaac Sim** and Webots, specifically configuring contact dynamics and closed-loop kinematic constraints to facilitate RL grasping policy training for multi-fingered dexterous hands.
* **Biomechanics & Embedded Control:** I designed an STM32-based embedded architecture and a motor-driven testbed for a human gait assistance system. This work successfully reduced metabolic walking cost by 20–30%, and a manuscript is currently in preparation for **IROS**.
* **Autonomous Navigation & Vision:** I engineered a custom **ROS2-based mobile manipulator** from scratch, integrating LiDAR-based SLAM, Nav2, and an Image-Based Visual Servoing pipeline to achieve high-precision dynamic grasping.

Ultimately, I aspire to pursue a Ph.D. in Robotics to continue pushing the boundaries of embodied AI and robust control.
